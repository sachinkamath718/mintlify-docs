### Step 4: Click on "**Continue to Launch**" then click on "**Launch**"

> Pre-Requisite: You should be logged in to your AWS account to perfrom next actions

You will be re-directed to **Cloudinformation Service** in your AWS account. Condese will be deployed here

### Step 5: CloudFormation is used to deploy the condense using IaC (cloud formation YAML template), which is fetched from the S3 URL.

![](/images/condense/condense-deployment/bring-your-own-cloud-byoc/files%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FvbacpakLuL4x2VBp2174%252Fuploads%252FoFtn7MSrfet3k97Npiwj%252FAWS%25204.png)

### Step 6: Click on "**Next"** and specify the details required for condense:

* **Stack Name** - This can be alphanumeric characters with a length of up to 128 characters.

* **VPC and subnet** - These are auto-populated with default values. You will have to cross-verify the VPC and Subnet CIDR range before proceeding with the deployment.

* **Instance Type** - By default, *m6a.xlarge* is mentioned, you can change this if required.

* **Email Address** - This is a mandatory field as well, which is used to receive emails from us. We will also share the login credentials to access the Condense Application here.

### Step 7: After adding and verifying the details, Click on “Next” and finally click on the “Submit“ button.

This will initiate the deployment of stacks in condense. Once Condense is deployed in your account, you will get an email from us with the login credentials to access the Condense App
