You can directly subscribe to condense via the marketplace in the GCP console.

### Step 1: Visit the [Google Cloud Marketplace](https://console.cloud.google.com/marketplace/product/zeliot-public/condense) and search for [Condense](https://console.cloud.google.com/marketplace/product/zeliot-public/condense)

![](https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/spaces%2FvbacpakLuL4x2VBp2174%2Fuploads%2FGTNci252FSHqce6IS0CV%2FGoogle%20Marketplace.png)

### Step 2: Click on "Subscribe"

![](https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/spaces%2FvbacpakLuL4x2VBp2174%2Fuploads%2FJhmM5QOUFh1fRsW35iFN%2Fsubscribe.png)

### Step 3: Adjust the timeframe, review terms and click on "**Subscribe**"

![](https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/spaces%2FvbacpakLuL4x2VBp2174%2Fuploads%2FIXLQtO9evMTagUf2BKs8%2FSubscribe%201.png)

### Step 4: Click on "Sign Up with Zeliot INC" to sign up and start using Condense

![](https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/spaces%2FvbacpakLuL4x2VBp2174%2Fuploads%2FsMLXQvds0d1jDn4KMcLc%2Fsign%20up%20with%20zeliot.png)

### Step 5: You're all set to start using Condense

The status and button on the GCP marketplace will be changed to "**Manage on Provider**" and the link to access the Condense App will be shared with your registered email address.
