#### Use Case: Real-Time Mining Vehicle Digitization Powered by Condense

The Mining Vehicle Digitization System is a real-time solution designed to bring full visibility, automation, and predictive intelligence to mining operations by streaming vehicle telemetry, driver behaviour, and operational activities in real time.

Built on Condense, the system ingests high-frequency data from mining trucks, excavators, and auxiliary equipment, processes events like location updates, load cycles, and tip sensor movements, and delivers real-time operational insights without the need for complex backend infrastructure.

Whenever a mining vehicle loads, dumps, or moves between zones, the system automatically logs the events, tracks turnaround times (TAT), monitors driver efficiency, and triggers smart alerts for anomalies. Predictive models analyze vehicle health and operational cycles continuously to optimize maintenance, safety, and production outputs.

#### Benefits

**End-to-End Fleet and Excavator Visibility**

- Provides real-time GPS tracking and dynamic geofencing of mining fleets and excavators.
- Visualizes loading, hauling, and dumping activities across rugged, multi-zone mining sites.

**Trip and Load Cycle Automation**

- Automatically detects and logs each load/unload trip using GPS entry/exit events and tip sensor signals.
- Generates accurate, real-time trip reports without manual intervention.

**Excavator-Centric TAT Monitoring**

- Measures the time taken for trucks to queue, load, and depart from excavators.
- Identifies bottlenecks, idle times, and productivity gaps across multiple shifts.

**AI-Driven Load and Performance Analytics**

- Integrates AI models to estimate load movement without the need for costly onboard load sensors.
- Enables real-time benchmarking of driver performance, excavator utilization, and fleet throughput.

**Intelligent Safety and Compliance Tracking**

- Sends automated alerts for unauthorized zone entries, idle overstays, or unsafe vehicle behavior.
- Maintains traceable safety compliance logs for audits and operational reviews.

**Scalable, Low-Complexity Deployment**

- Built on Condense’s fully managed, cloud-native platform, with elastic scalability across mining operations.
- Operates with a BYOC (Bring Your Own Cloud) model, ensuring full data sovereignty and security.
