1. [GETTING STARTED](/condense/v2.4.0/getting-started)

Condense is an all-in-one, complete data streaming platform powered by fully managed Kafka that is deployable on customer cloud environments through fully managed BYOC (Bring Your Own Cloud). It comes with a robust verticalized ecosystem, transforming and accelerating the development of streaming applications like never before.

![](/images/condense/v2.4.0/getting-started/files%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FrwKRGO3QthZ6EMqqYblg%252Fuploads%252F29kkWst8WyeMCd1IrcOd%252FIntrodcution%2520Bento.png)

## Key Features of Condense

### ![](/images/condense/v2.4.0/getting-started/files%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FrwKRGO3QthZ6EMqqYblg%252Fuploads%252F5MOJ2Muls45wctEJbgvy%252Fimage.png) Complete Data Streaming Platform

A fully managed Kafka and streaming platform designed for real time impact.

Go beyond Kafka. Condense offers prebuilt connectors, stream governance, enterprise-grade security, and an intuitive data pipeline approach.

Dealing with real-time data shouldn’t mean juggling multiple tools. Our unique combination eliminates the complexity, giving you all the tools needed for a complete data streaming solution.

### ![](/images/condense/v2.4.0/getting-started/files%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FrwKRGO3QthZ6EMqqYblg%252Fuploads%252FnIbyqgnI5042Kbl9SROi%252Fimage.png) Complex Infrastructure, Simplified

Condense simplifies infrastructure management by automating Kafka, Kubernetes, and other backend services.

With built-in security and real-time observability, you can monitor and optimize your system effortlessly.

### ![](/images/condense/v2.4.0/getting-started/files%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FrwKRGO3QthZ6EMqqYblg%252Fuploads%252FEbMhk3TqrrcGVyrCESaN%252Fimage.png) Accelerated real time data processing.

Design and deploy data pipelines effortlessly using prebuilt transforms, marketplace options, or custom-built transforms tailored to your needs.

Accelerate transformations, deliver precise outputs, and realize your use cases faster.

### ![](/images/condense/v2.4.0/getting-started/files%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FrwKRGO3QthZ6EMqqYblg%252Fuploads%252FOO7lTekRawWPBg61DwRV%252Fimage.png) Continuous Data Workflows, Uninterrupted

Condense ensures uninterrupted execution of transforms for continuous streaming data processing, delivering precise outputs to your applications. From hosting to running your applications, Condense manages the entire backend. Whether it’s handling peak loads or maintaining stability for critical workflows.

### ![](/images/condense/v2.4.0/getting-started/files%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FrwKRGO3QthZ6EMqqYblg%252Fuploads%252FWOY8bHSpQd2GRIO8CeAG%252Fimage.png) Your Cloud, Your Data, Your Rules

Condense is deployed on your cloud infrastructure, ensuring all data from your business sources stays within your environment under your full ownership. With Condense’s fully managed Bring Your Own Cloud (BYOC) model, you retain 100% control of your data without the complexity of managing the system. Enjoy security, scalability, and simplicity, all while empowering your business to innovate with confidence.
