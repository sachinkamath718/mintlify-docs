### Overview

Have a question, need technical help, or want to discuss a custom use case? Reach out to the Condense team directly via email — we're happy to help.

---

### Email Contacts

#### 🛠️ Technical Support

For any technical issues, bugs, or questions about setting up Condense:

📧 [support@zeliot.in](mailto:support@zeliot.in)

#### 💼 Sales & Business Enquiries

For pricing, plans, enterprise options, or product discussions:

📧 [sales@zeliot.in](mailto:sales@zeliot.in)

#### 👋 General Enquiries

For anything else — partnerships, feedback, or general questions:

📧 [hello@zeliot.in](mailto:hello@zeliot.in)

---

### What to Include in Your Email

To help us resolve your query faster, please include the following in your message:

- **Subject line** — a brief description of your issue or request
- **Your Organization / Account name** on Condense
- **Workspace name** (if the issue is workspace-specific)
- **Description** of the issue or request — include steps to reproduce if it's a bug
- **Screenshots or logs** if applicable

---

For urgent technical issues, we recommend raising a support ticket directly from the Condense App for faster tracking and resolution. See [How to Raise a Ticket](/condense/v2.4.0/help-and-support/how-to-raise-a-ticket).
