# Demo Workspaces

Demo Workspaces provide a guided, hands-on environment for exploring Condense capabilities without building a solution from scratch. Each workspace includes pre-configured connectors, transforms, pipelines, dashboards, and sample data to help users understand real-world streaming use cases.

Introduced in **Condense v2.4.0**, Demo Workspaces enable users to experience the platform with ready-to-use applications that demonstrate common streaming patterns and industry scenarios.

## Overview

Each Demo Workspace is designed around a specific business problem and contains everything required to explore the solution, including:

- Pre-configured pipelines
- Sample streaming data
- Ready-to-use connectors
- Predefined transforms
- Kafka topics
- Interactive dashboards (where applicable)

Demo Workspaces can be explored without modifying the underlying configuration, making them suitable for product evaluations, learning, and demonstrations.

## Launching a Demo Workspace

1. Sign in to Condense.
2. Navigate to **Demo Workspaces**.
3. Select the required workspace.
4. Click **Launch**.
5. Wait for the workspace to be provisioned.
6. Explore the pre-configured resources and data pipelines.

> **Note**
>
> Provisioning time may vary depending on the selected workspace and available infrastructure.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do Demo Workspaces modify my existing workspaces?

No. Demo Workspaces are provisioned independently and do not affect your existing workspaces.

### Can I modify a Demo Workspace?

Yes. Depending on your permissions, you can explore and modify resources within the Demo Workspace.

### Are Demo Workspaces intended for production use?

No. Demo Workspaces are designed for learning, evaluation, demonstrations, and proof-of-concept scenarios.

### Do Demo Workspaces include sample data?

Yes. Each workspace includes sample data and pre-configured resources required to demonstrate the use case.
