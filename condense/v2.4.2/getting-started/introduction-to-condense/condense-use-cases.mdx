Condense caters to a broad spectrum of organizations that utilize mobility devices to collect data. This includes:

![Cover](/images/condense/v2.4.0/getting-started/introduction-to-condense/rwKRGO3QthZ6EMqqYblg%2Fblobs%2FkWnHHq5m6oGisivg3Q7c%2FAutomotive%2520Man.png)

### Automotive Manufacuturers

Leverage Condense for data collection from connected vehicles, enabling analysis of fuel efficiency, driver behavior, and vehicle performance.

![Cover](/images/condense/v2.4.0/getting-started/introduction-to-condense/rwKRGO3QthZ6EMqqYblg%2Fblobs%2Fctd7WUxnXnU7k1NNnll4%2FTelematics%2520Service.png)

### Telematics Service Providers

Condense empowers telematics providers to offer a robust data ingestion platform to their customers, allowing seamless data collection from diverse mobility devices.

![Cover](/images/condense/v2.4.0/getting-started/introduction-to-condense/rwKRGO3QthZ6EMqqYblg%2Fblobs%2FpTLKtT1EewGtK7MA47sd%2FConstruction%2520Equipment.png)

### Construction & Heavy Equipment Industries

Condense simplifies data collection from construction machinery, enabling monitoring of equipment performance, fuel consumption, and maintenance schedules.

![Cover](/images/condense/v2.4.0/getting-started/introduction-to-condense/rwKRGO3QthZ6EMqqYblg%2Fblobs%2F8NQil5pZcBbku6KuI8ri%2FSmart%2520City.png)

### Smart Cities & Urban Infrastructure Management

Condense can be used to collect data from sensors deployed in cities, providing platform to develop custom application to get insights into traffic patterns, environmental conditions, and resource utilization.

![Cover](/images/condense/v2.4.0/getting-started/introduction-to-condense/rwKRGO3QthZ6EMqqYblg%2Fblobs%2FqlQFGBUvSgQLhJTj1aK5%2FLogistics.png)

### Logistics & Transportation Companies

Condense facilitates efficient data acquisition from fleet vehicles, companies can develop custom applications to get insights into route optimization, driver safety, and engine health.
