title: "Upgrade"
---

Upgrade your Condense evaluation workspace to continue using the platform with production-ready deployments, additional resources, and enterprise capabilities.

The upgrade process allows users who started with the **Try Condense for Free** experience to seamlessly transition to a standard Condense organization without losing their account.

## Overview

Users who sign up through the **Try Condense for Free** experience are provisioned with a demo organization and assigned the **Demo Developer** role. When they decide to upgrade, they can create or join a standard Condense organization using the same account. Demo users are not restricted to a single organization and can continue accessing their evaluation environment while working in their upgraded organization

## Upgrade Process

The upgrade journey consists of the following steps:

1. Sign in to your existing Condense account.
2. Select **Upgrade** from the Console.
3. Choose the appropriate deployment or subscription option.
4. Complete the required organization and deployment details.
5. Continue using Condense under your upgraded organization.

## What Changes After Upgrade

After upgrading:

- Your Condense account remains the same.
- You can join or create a standard Condense organization.
- Your evaluation (demo) organization remains available.
- You are no longer limited to the evaluation experience and can use standard Condense organization workflows.

## Eligibility

You can upgrade if you have:

- A valid Condense account created through the **Try Condense for Free** experience.
- Completed the account activation process.
