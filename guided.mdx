The **Guided Tour** helps first-time users quickly become familiar with the Condense platform by providing an interactive walkthrough of the key areas and workflows.

The tour is designed to reduce the learning curve and help users begin building real-time streaming applications as quickly as possible.

## When is the Guided Tour Available?

The Guided Tour is automatically available when you access your trial environment for the first time.

You can choose to:

- Start the tour immediately.
- Skip the tour and explore the platform on your own.
- Restart the tour later, if available.

## What You'll Learn

The Guided Tour introduces the core capabilities of Condense, including:

- Platform navigation
- Workspaces
- Applications
- Stream Processing
- Kafka Management
- Connectors
- Demo Workspaces

Each step provides contextual information about the selected feature and guides you through the platform.

## Learning Through Exploration

The Guided Tour is intended to complement hands-on exploration. As you progress through the tour, you can interact with the platform, explore available resources, and become familiar with common workflows.

After completing the tour, you can continue exploring the available demo workspaces or begin building your own streaming applications.
