---
title: "Trial Experience"
---

The **Try Condense for Free** experience is designed to help you explore the core capabilities of Condense through a guided, hands-on evaluation. Once your trial environment is provisioned, you can immediately begin exploring the platform without any additional configuration.

Your trial environment is pre-configured with sample resources, guided onboarding, and demo applications to help you understand how Condense enables real-time data streaming and application development.

## What You Can Explore

During your trial, you can:

- Navigate the Condense platform through an interactive guided tour.
- Explore pre-configured demo workspaces.
- Build and deploy stream processing applications.
- Manage Kafka resources through the Kafka Management interface.
- Configure connectors and processing utilities.
- Understand the end-to-end application development and deployment workflow.

## Trial Workspace

Each trial environment includes a default **Quickstart** workspace designed to help you begin exploring the platform immediately.

The workspace includes:

- Sample applications.
- Pre-configured Kafka topics.
- Demo resources for learning platform capabilities.

## Guided Onboarding

The first time you sign in, Condense guides you through the platform using an interactive walkthrough.

The guided onboarding introduces key areas of the platform, including:

- Navigation
- Workspaces
- Kafka Management
- Applications
- Stream Processing
- Demo Workspaces

The guided tour can be skipped and revisited later.

## Demo Applications

The trial includes interactive demo applications that showcase common real-time streaming use cases.

These applications are designed to help you understand platform capabilities through practical examples before building your own applications.

## Collaborating with Your Team

You can invite additional users to collaborate within the same trial environment, enabling teams to evaluate Condense together.

## Upgrading Your Trial

When you are ready to move beyond the evaluation experience, you can upgrade your trial environment to a standard Condense deployment using the same Condense account.

For more information, see **Upgrade**.
