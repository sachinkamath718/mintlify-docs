---
title: "AI Agents in Condense"
---

AI Agents are integrated into the Condense platform to simplify platform operations and improve observability across your streaming infrastructure. They work alongside existing platform capabilities, helping teams monitor system health and gain operational insights without leaving the Condense interface.

Rather than replacing existing monitoring tools, AI Agents complement them by providing contextual assistance and surfacing relevant operational information across supported services.

## How AI Agents Fit into Condense

AI Agents operate across different layers of the Condense platform.

```text
Condense Platform
│
├── Applications
├── Stream Processing
├── Kafka Management
├── Connectors
├── Infrastructure
└── AI Agents
     ├── Kafka Monitoring
     ├── Kubernetes Monitoring
     └── Grafana Monitoring
```

Each AI Agent focuses on a specific operational domain while contributing to a unified monitoring experience.

## Supported Monitoring Areas

AI Agents currently provide monitoring capabilities for:

- Apache Kafka
- Kubernetes
- Grafana

Support for additional platform components may be introduced in future releases.

## Benefits

AI Agents help platform teams to:

- Monitor platform health.
- Improve operational visibility.
- Identify infrastructure issues more efficiently.
- Reduce the effort required to monitor distributed streaming environments.
