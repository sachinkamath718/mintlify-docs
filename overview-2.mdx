Condense AI Agents are intelligent assistants designed to simplify the operation and management of real-time streaming platforms. They help engineering teams monitor platform health, identify operational issues, and provide contextual insights across different platform components.

AI Agents continuously analyze operational data and surface relevant information, allowing teams to respond to infrastructure events more efficiently and reduce the operational overhead associated with managing distributed streaming environments.

AI Agents are integrated into the Condense platform and work alongside existing monitoring and management capabilities.

## Available AI Agents

Condense currently provides AI Agents for the following platform components:

| AI Agent | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Kafka Monitoring Agent** | Monitors Apache Kafka clusters and provides operational insights into brokers, topics, partitions, consumer groups, and cluster health. |
| **Kubernetes Monitoring Agent** | Monitors Kubernetes resources supporting the Condense platform, helping identify workload and infrastructure issues. |
| **Grafana Monitoring Agent** | Assists in interpreting monitoring dashboards and infrastructure metrics to improve operational visibility. |

Additional AI Agents may be introduced in future releases.

## Key Capabilities

AI Agents help you:

- Monitor platform health.
- Identify operational issues.
- Analyze infrastructure metrics.
- Improve platform observability.
- Reduce operational troubleshooting time.
