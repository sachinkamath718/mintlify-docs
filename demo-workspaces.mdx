---
title: "Demo Workspaces"
---

Demo Workspaces provide a hands-on way to explore Condense through pre-configured applications and real-world streaming scenarios. Each workspace showcases specific platform capabilities, enabling you to learn by interacting with working pipelines instead of building everything from scratch.

The workspaces are designed to help you understand how data flows through Condense, how stream processing applications are built, and how different platform components work together.

## What's Included

Depending on your trial environment, demo workspaces may include:

- Pre-configured streaming pipelines.
- Sample data sources.
- Kafka topics.
- Connectors and transformations.
- Interactive applications.
- Guided learning experiences.

Each workspace is fully configured and ready to explore.

## Learning Objectives

Demo Workspaces help you learn how to:

- Build real-time streaming pipelines.
- Process and transform streaming data.
- Configure connectors.
- Explore Kafka Management capabilities.
- Understand common streaming architectures.
- Navigate the Condense platform.

## Available Workspaces

The available demo workspaces may vary depending on the version of Condense.

Examples include:

- Auto Twin
- Spot the Fraudster

Additional workspaces may be introduced in future releases.

## Explore and Experiment

You are encouraged to explore the available workspaces, inspect the configured resources, and understand how different platform components work together.

The demo workspaces are intended as learning resources and can be used as a reference while building your own applications.
