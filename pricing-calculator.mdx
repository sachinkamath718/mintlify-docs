title: "Pricing Calculator"
---

The Pricing Calculator helps you estimate the cost of your Condense deployment based on your selected configuration and pricing model. It provides an estimate of your subscription before deployment, allowing you to understand the expected cost for your environment.

The calculator is intended for planning purposes and provides pricing estimates based on the selected deployment configuration.

## Why Use the Pricing Calculator?

The Pricing Calculator helps you:

- Estimate deployment costs before provisioning.
- Compare different deployment configurations.
- Understand the impact of infrastructure sizing.
- Evaluate pricing across supported pricing models.
- Plan your subscription based on business and technical requirements.

## Supported Pricing Models

The Pricing Calculator supports all pricing models available for your organization, including:

- Flat Pricing
- Tiered Pricing

Pricing estimates are generated using the pricing model applicable to your selected deployment.

## Estimate Your Deployment

When creating an estimate, you can configure deployment parameters such as:

- Cloud provider
- Deployment model
- Cluster configuration
- Infrastructure sizing
- Pricing model

The estimated cost is updated based on the selected configuration.

> **Note:** The Pricing Calculator provides an estimate only. Actual billing is based on your organization's subscription and commercial agreement.
