AI Agents are integrated into the Condense platform to assist with platform monitoring and operational visibility.

Once AI Agents are available for your organization, they can be accessed directly from the supported areas of the Condense platform.

## Before You Begin

Before using AI Agents, ensure that:

- You have access to a Condense organization.
- Your organization has AI Agents enabled.
- You have the necessary permissions to access the monitored resources.

## Supported Services

AI Agents currently support monitoring for:

- Apache Kafka
- Kubernetes
- Grafana

Additional agents may be introduced in future releases.

## Getting Started

Once enabled, AI Agents automatically analyze supported platform resources and provide contextual operational insights within the Condense platform.

No additional installation or configuration is required for supported services.
